## 2. Core Architecture
The project is built at the intersection of the most decentralized Cypherpunk protocols available today:

* **Sovereign Identity:** No Emails, No Passwords. Every Node authenticates via **Nostr (NIP-07)**. The power remains in your Private Key.
* **Encrypted Commerce:** Our Eco-Marketplace utilizes **NIP-44 End-to-End Encryption**. Negotiation and commerce remain entirely anonymous and peer-to-peer.
* **Decentralized Storage:** "Proof of Nature" (biomass imagery/audio) is hashed and stored across **IPFS / Arweave / Shadow Drive**. 
* **The Value & Energy Layer:** Utilizing **Bitcoin L2 / RGB Protocol** for asset minting (USDG) and client-side validation. Micro-payments and gas fees are routed through the **Lightning Network** (NWC compatible).

## 3. Core Modules
- `[ROOTING]` **Onboarding Flow:** Connect Nostr keys and cryptographically sign the "Genesis Covenant."
- `[FEED]` **The Biomass Feed:** Where Nodes upload their Proof of Nature. Every piece of media is timestamped and immutably tied to a pubkey.
- `[MARKET]` **Eco-Commerce:** A decentralized marketplace for verified Nodes to offer Eco-Stays or Harvests, exchanging value in USDG and Sats.
- `[DAO]` **The Vetting Board:** Operated via a "Web of Trust." A new Node must prove at least 21 days of continuous Proof of Nature on the Feed and receive enough "Vouches" from the community to unlock commerce privileges.
